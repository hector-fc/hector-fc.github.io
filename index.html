About

I am a Professor at the Department of Mathematics at the University of Mato Grosso (UFMT) in Brazil. My research interests include Mathematical Optimization, Numerical Methods, and Mathematical Modeling.

Research Interests

  • Numerical Optimization
  • Global Optimization
  • Mathematical Modeling in Science and Engineering

Education

  • PhD. University of Campinas, São Paulo - Brazil
  • MSc. University Federal of Fluminense, Rio de Janeiro - Brasil
  • Graduate in Mathematics, University Mayor de San Andres, La Paz - Bolivia
